Yog-Sothoth is both the key and the gate, as Randolph Carter would one day discover, and once one passes through the Gates of Wisdom, all manner of unnatural avenues open up.

"Only a few students of forbidden things have gained inklings of this control, and have thereby conquered time and change. But the entities outside the Gates command all angles, and view the myriad parts of the cosmos in terms of fragmentary, change-involving perspective, or of the changeless totality beyond perspective, in accordance with their will."
