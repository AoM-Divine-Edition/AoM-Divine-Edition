True dedication, devotion, is the next stage of a people enamoured by the ancient powers. Far from idle worship, now all will consider serving their highest calling.

"For The White Silence, we bake our bread. For The White Silence, we fatten our flesh. For The White Silence, we drink our blood. For The White Silence, we provide our young. May he love us in his mercy, and may he not visit us in the cold night."
