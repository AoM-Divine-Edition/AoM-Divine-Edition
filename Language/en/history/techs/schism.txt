Though Azathoth remains blessedly inert, the manifestations of his will are apparent, and his followers may beckon a sliver of his presence insofar as a circle of truly unmatched force.

Naturally, any attempts to surpass this court disaster of the most fundamental kind.  For Azathoth to awaken is for the dream that is our blissfully kind and gentle reality to end.
