
Made from wood and flint the knife handle represents an Aztec warrior. The handle of the knife has been carved to resemble a crouching figure who wears the ritual costume of an Aztec eagle-warrior or cuauhtliocelotl, one of the most senior Aztec military ranks and also translated as eagle-jaguar.The ceremonial knife or ixcuac measures approximately 32 cm in length and 10 cm in height.
