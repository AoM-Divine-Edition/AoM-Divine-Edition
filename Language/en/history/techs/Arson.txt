Xiuhtecuhtli was known as the lord of fire. He fathered various other deities associated with fire these include:  Xantico, Xiuhxoxoauhqui (blue fire), Xiuhcozauhqui (yellow fire), Xiuhiztac (white fire) and Xiuhtlatlauhqui (red fire). A small fire was permanently kept alive at the sacred center of every Aztec home in honor of Xiuhtecuhtli
