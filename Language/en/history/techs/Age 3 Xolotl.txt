The twin brother of Quetzalcoatl. He was commonly depicted as a dog-headed man and was a soul-guide for the dead. He was also a God of twins, monsters, misfortune, sickness, and deformities. 
When the gods decided to sacrifice themselves for the newly created sun. Xolotl withdrew from this sacrifice and wept so much his eyes fell out of their sockets.
One day Xolotl discovered a Cacao tree and wanted to keep its fruits for himself. But Quetzalcoatl saw the Cacao tree and shared it with the humankind.
