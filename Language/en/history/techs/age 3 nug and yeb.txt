Nug and Yeb are twin Great Old Ones, born from Shub-Niggurath, progenitors of such horrors as Cthulhu and Tsathoggua, and are entrusted with preparing the Earth for the Great Ones' return.

They live in the dark recesses of space, and rarely, if ever, interact with their worshippers in any real way, though they did often bless Irem with dubious gifts. 

"...The Spaniard watched the subtle orgiastic rites with fascination and repulsion. The ceremonies of Nug and Yeb sickened him especially - so much, indeed, that he refrained from describing them in his manuscript..."
