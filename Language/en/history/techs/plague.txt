Hastur's arrivals are always precidented by terrible plagues.  His followers, therefore, often seek to spread and manifest such pestilences, breeding virulent diseases in secret and augmenting them with eldritch arts.

Such a plague is this: the afflicted become more mushroom than man, condemned to wander until death, spreading noxious spores wherever they go.  When you see their hordes on the horizon, barricade your doors and slaughter the exposed before the infection can take root, lest your people become utterly consumed.  But remember: your enemies are but mortal too.  If your empire is lost to the plague, spread it far and wide yourself, so that your enemies may suffer as you have suffered.
