A wretched corpse rebirthed by Shub-Niggurath out of one of her gaping maws or by one of her Voices. Short-lived and weak, but a somewhat effective guardian.

"The mud-covered thing crawled at me, moaning in a voice most horrible. I knew it as Richards, but I knew not how. I drew my rifle to my sholder and fired, to no avail. Then my manhood left me, and I fled. I know not what became of it, or whether it still hunts me to this day."
