Once built, a ritual of farsight can be activated at any time, granting a flash of inspiration that reveals all of existence in a mighty burst of vision. Such things were originally the purview of Yog-Sothoth's high priests, but in these troubled times, it falls to mere cultists to construct and operate them.

"Now, intoxicated with wider visions, he scarcely knew what he sought. Thoughts of infinite and blasphemous daring rose in his mind, and he knew he would face the dreaded Guide without fear, asking monstrous and terrible things of him."
