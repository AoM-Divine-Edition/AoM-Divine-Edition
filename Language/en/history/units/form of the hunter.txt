Cultists of Ithaqua can undergo a transformation not entirely unlike the far, far more significant transformation of non-eldritch Wendigo. Their bodies become filled with a tiny sliver of Ithaqua's icy spirit, and it twists and warps their bodies into ideal hunters, able to both fight and hunt their prey in relative safety.
