This poor thing is a fungus-encrusted victim of Hastur's greatest pestilence. Still alive and in incredible pain, this infectious thing crawls mindlessly around until its body gives out. Should one approach you, it's best to simply kill yourself. Have been known to mindlessly attack nearby units and buildings, churning up clouds of disease.
