In some cases, Azathoth himself is likened to a sapient, sleeping black hole at the center of the universe. This description is of course insufficient, but fragments of that astrophysical force can be summoned to terrible effect.

"I saw it, I tell you, not in a dream but in my waking mind, the visage of the Demiurge himself, out there among the stars. A blackness, a blackness deeper than the void of space, and around it the stars themselves spun and were torn apart as they fell ever-closer to that accursed thing."
